
Naming Conventions
Scripts > SCR_Name
FBX Files > MDL_FBX_Name
Generalised Models > MDL_Name
Materials > MAT_Name
Shaders > Shader_Name
Shader Graph > Shader_Graph_Name
Scenes > Scene_Name
